I was born in Dunedin, New Zealand where I lived with my parents and two older brothers. My family moved to Wānaka in Central Otago in 1999, where we lived for the next ten years. I attended Bayfield High School from 2009 - 2013. I was heavily involved in sports throughout high school - I played netball, basketball, and volleyball.
